The Hope

Coming 2025 Short Film Drama

When Sarah's world crumbles after losing her job and facing eviction, she finds herself questioning everything she once believed. But through an unexpected encounter with a homeless veteran who radiates peace despite his circumstances, she discovers that true hope isn't found in our situations, but in something far greater. A powerful story about finding light in the darkest moments and learning that God's love never fails, even when everything else does.
